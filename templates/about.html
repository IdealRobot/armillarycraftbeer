The Story Behind The Beer

About

Built on experience, community, and a love for great beer, Armillary Brewing brings together craft brewing, welcoming spaces, and a little cosmic chaos.

The Founders

Jeff Lockhart

Co-Owner / Brewer

Jeff Lockhart is a longtime Colorado craft brewer with more than 15 years of experience in the industry. Before opening Armillary Brewing, he helped launch multiple breweries and brewed at several others throughout Colorado’s evolving craft beer scene.

Kristin Lockhart

Co-Owner

Kristin helped shape Armillary Brewing around the idea of creating a welcoming neighborhood brewery where people can gather, relax, share food, and enjoy the experience together.

Built For Community

Neighborhood First

The Lockharts wanted Armillary Brewing to be more than a beer stop. Their vision was a comfortable place where families, friends, and regulars could settle in for the evening.

The Armillary Approach

Craft Beer

Colorado Brewed

Armillary Brewing focuses on balanced, approachable, small-batch beers inspired by Colorado’s strong craft brewing culture.

Beer & Pizza

Simple Pairing

Having food onsite was always part of the plan. Artisan pizzas help create a full taproom experience where guests can stay, relax, and enjoy the space together.

Cosmic Energy

Armillary Brewing

From the branding to the atmosphere, Armillary Brewing embraces creativity, exploration, and the feeling that something interesting is always happening around the next corner.
